# User Personas — AI-Powered Banking & Financial Assistant

---

## 1. Overview

This document defines the primary and secondary user personas for the AI-powered banking assistant. These personas help in designing user-centric features, improving usability, and aligning the AI system with real-world needs.

---

## 2. Persona 1: Retail Banking Customer

### Profile:

* Age: 25–45
* Occupation: Salaried Employee
* Technical Skill: Low to Medium

### Goals:

* Manage monthly expenses
* Save money effectively
* Get quick financial advice

### Pain Points:

* Difficulty tracking expenses
* Lack of financial knowledge
* Confusion in savings and investment options

### How AI Helps:

* Provides spending insights
* Suggests savings plans
* Answers financial queries in simple language

### Example Query:

"How can I save more money every month?"

---

## 3. Persona 2: Loan Seeker

### Profile:

* Age: 28–50
* Occupation: Salaried / Self-employed
* Technical Skill: Medium

### Goals:

* Understand loan options
* Calculate EMI before applying
* Choose best repayment plan

### Pain Points:

* Complex loan terms
* Lack of clarity on EMI calculations
* Fear of hidden costs

### How AI Helps:

* Explains loan structure
* Provides EMI breakdown
* Suggests optimal tenure

### Example Query:

"What will be my EMI for a 20 lakh loan?"

---

## 4. Persona 3: Beginner Investor

### Profile:

* Age: 22–35
* Occupation: Young Professional
* Technical Skill: Medium

### Goals:

* Start investing
* Understand financial products
* Minimize risk

### Pain Points:

* Lack of investment knowledge
* Fear of losses
* Too many options in market

### How AI Helps:

* Suggests beginner-friendly investment options
* Explains risk vs return
* Provides simple comparisons

### Example Query:

"Where should I invest 50,000?"

---

## 5. Persona 4: Small Business Owner

### Profile:

* Age: 30–55
* Occupation: Entrepreneur / SME Owner
* Technical Skill: Medium

### Goals:

* Manage business expenses
* Improve profitability
* Optimize cash flow

### Pain Points:

* High operational costs
* Lack of financial insights
* Manual tracking of expenses

### How AI Helps:

* Provides expense analysis
* Suggests cost optimization strategies
* Helps in financial planning

### Example Query:

"How can I reduce my business expenses?"

---

## 6. Persona 5: Financial Analyst (Secondary User)

### Profile:

* Age: 25–40
* Occupation: Analyst / Finance Professional
* Technical Skill: High

### Goals:

* Quick insights
* Data interpretation
* Decision support

### Pain Points:

* Time-consuming analysis
* Manual reporting

### How AI Helps:

* Provides summarized insights
* Speeds up decision-making
* Acts as an assistant tool

### Example Query:

"Give me a quick summary of spending patterns"

---

## 7. Key Insights from Personas

* Users prefer **simple, conversational interaction**
* Trust and accuracy are critical in financial domain
* Personalization is a key differentiator
* Users want **actionable insights, not just data**

---

## 8. Design Implications

Based on personas, the system should:

* Use simple and clear language
* Avoid technical jargon
* Provide structured outputs (bullets, summaries)
* Include disclaimers for financial advice
* Ensure fast response time

---

## 9. Summary

These personas ensure that:

* The AI system is user-centric
* Features align with real needs
* Product decisions are data-driven

They directly influence:

* UI/UX design
* Model behavior
* Feature prioritization
